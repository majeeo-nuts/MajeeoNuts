Return & Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation is allowed within 1–2 days if the item hasn't been shipped.
  • No cancellation for perishable items unless quality is proven poor.
  • Damaged/defective items must be reported within 1–2 days of delivery.
  • If not satisfied with the product, raise a complaint within 1–2 days.
  • Refunds (if approved) will take 6–8 days to reflect in your account.
  • Warranty-related complaints should be taken up with the manufacturer directly.
